About Cell Types
  • Sensitive Cells

    Cancer cells that respond well to treatment but proliferate quickly.

  • Resistant Cells

    Cancer cells that have evolved resistance to treatment but grow more slowly.

  • Cancer Stem Cells

    Highly resilient cells that can self-renew and regenerate tumor mass.

Evolutionary Dynamics

This simulation is based on evolutionary game theory applied to cancer:

  • Cells compete for resources within the tumor
  • Fitness is determined by interactions between cell types
  • Mutations allow cells to change type over time
  • Drug pressure creates selection for resistant phenotypes
  • Some randomness (chaos) models unpredictable biological events
Treatment Strategies

Explore different treatment approaches:

  • Maximum Tolerated Dose: High drug strength, high decay
  • Metronomic Therapy: Moderate strength, low decay
  • Adaptive Therapy: Adjust drug based on response
  • Immune Support: Increase immune strength with drug

Challenge: Can you find parameters that lead to complete tumor eradication?
